About

My name is Victor Toso de Carvalho and I’m a Brazilian Software Engineer based in the city of Colle di Val d’Elsa, Italy.

I’m currently working in the Virtualization team of Red Hat in projects that relate to QEMU Libvirt and KubeVirt. I’ve also worked a few years in the SPICE project together with the Red Hat’s Desktop team.

I’ve been a proud member of the GNOME community and a foundation member since 2014.
